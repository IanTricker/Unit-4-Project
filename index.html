RENNALA QUEEN OF THE FULL MOON

Renalla, Queen of the Full Moon from Elden Ring, is a regal and enigmatic figure characterized by her ethereal beauty and mysterious presence. She is associated with the lunar aspect, embodying the moon's influence over the world of Elden Ring. Renalla exudes an aura of both grace and power, often depicted as a pivotal figure in the game's lore, shaping events with her celestial authority. Her story intertwines with the game's themes of darkness and light, offering players a compelling glimpse into the mythos of Elden Ring through her character.

STARSCOURGE RADAHN

Radahn from Elden Ring is a formidable and imposing figure, known as the Elden Lord. Clad in heavy armor adorned with intricate designs, he wields a massive sword that embodies his immense strength and martial prowess. Radahn commands respect and fear on the battlefield, standing as a formidable adversary in the player's journey through the lands of Elden Ring. His presence signifies a significant challenge and a key figure in the unfolding epic tale of the game.

MORGOTT THE OMEN KING

Morgott the Omen King from Elden Ring is a dark and foreboding figure, draped in ominous robes that hint at his mystical and malevolent nature. He is surrounded by an aura of prophecy and doom, wielding arcane powers that manipulate fate itself. Morgott's presence looms large in the game's narrative, casting a shadow over the world of Elden Ring with his enigmatic and potentially cataclysmic intentions.

GODRICK THE GRAFTED

Godrick from Elden Ring is a colossal and grotesque figure, adorned with ancient armor fused with the remains of his defeated enemies. He possesses an unsettling mix of regality and decay, embodying a tragic figure cursed with eternal hunger. Godrick's imposing stature and relentless pursuit of sustenance make him a formidable and haunting presence in the game's world, serving as both a powerful boss and a poignant symbol of fallen greatness.
